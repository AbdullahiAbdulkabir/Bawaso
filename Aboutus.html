About Us

Our passion at Bawaso.com is first to satisfy our Client from all walks of life by providing an easy and convenient platform to achieve their desired needs.

Who We Are.

Bawaso Limited is a brand communication and talent marketing company dedicated to promoting and showcasing Africa’s talent and human resources to the world. At Bawaso, we constantly maintain an open line of communication and accessibility to creative minds and human resources.

Bawaso.com is not just an online platform connecting Talents and Brands, we also aim at breaking down the cumbersome and long process of securing specialized services in the Entertainment Industry. We match brands to top talents & trend-setters that can reach the world via endorsements, publicity initiatives, performance, promotional programs, social media outreach, integrations and/or content development.

In addition, we believe all events are special memories worth keeping and reliving. We therefore help our esteemed clients prepare and plan the event ahead so they can enjoy the happiness of reliving the event. We also recommend talents that suits our clients’ needs thereby making their event memorable and unforgettable.

Our Mission.

Setting up a virtual platform aimed at connecting creative talent to the world.
